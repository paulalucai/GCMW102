About Me

Originally from Boston, I moved here to Southern California 15 years ago so that my family could escape the freezing cold winters. However, unlike my family, I love the cold and the snow and I miss being in New England. Oh yeah, and go Patriots!


I like to label myself as a hybrid between a geek and a nerd, as I love video games, tech, anime, and learning. But I also have a very girly side; I'm really into fashion and beauty. It seems to me that I'm a unicorn, as I'm the only one of my kind that I know of!
